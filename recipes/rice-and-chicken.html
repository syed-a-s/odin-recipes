Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice - important
  • Chicken
  • Onions

Instructions

  1. Cut onions
  2. Cook onions, taking care to add water every now and then
  3. When onions are transluscent, add spices. Turmeric, Chili Pepper, Cumin
  4. Cook onions until pretty much dissolved
  5. Add chicken
  6. Cook chicken, taking care to add water every now and then
  7. Don't forget salt
  8. While the chicken cooks, wash your rice - important
  9. Fill rice cooker with 1 part rice to 1 part water
  10. Wait
  11. Serve
